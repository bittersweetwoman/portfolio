About



My portfolio also includes various creative arts illustrations, and graphic content. I am proficient in narrative writing, as well as art writing and criticism, bringing a nuanced and insightful perspective to my analysis of visual arts. Whether I'm working on a project as a writer, artist, or curator, I always bring my passion for creative expression and my dedication to excellence to every endeavor.


Creative Writing

As a creative writer, curator, and visual artist, I specialize in creating spaces that provoke introspection, contemplation, and empathy. I believe in the power of art to evoke emotions, inspire new ways of thinking, and facilitate healing, and my work reflects this ethos. My writing focuses on mental health and the intricacies of our inner lives, and I approach these themes with nuance, sensitivity, and care. I am the author of a self-published novel titled Bittersweet Woman and a poetry memoir centered around mental wellness and trauma healing. I have also collaborated with mental health professionals, collectives, and organizations to create impactful content that resonates with readers.

Visual Art

As a visual artist, I use narrative storytelling through images. I offer licensing of my images for website and blog use, and I am always eager to collaborate with clients on creative projects that push the boundaries of what's possible.

Curatorial Practice

As a Curator my vision is to curate experiences that go beyond the visual—where art becomes a dialogue, a shared journey, and an emotional connection. My primary passion lies in preserving and telling the stories of others, using empathy as both a guiding tool and a source of impetus for creation. This commitment informs my approach to curating, as I endeavor to shape environments where stories can not only exist, but also resonate with those who encounter them.

Narrative Art & Curatorial Practice


Narrative art is not just about presenting images, but about telling stories—stories that reflect personal, cultural, and universal human experiences. Through my academic and professional journey in curation and creative writing, I’ve sought to explore and curate stories that resonate with the human condition. My work bridges the gap between written and visual storytelling, where each project serves to inspire empathy, healing, and connection. Explore the journey that began with Bittersweet Woman, a personal narrative on trauma, recovery, and growth.
